Our Mission

At Gardeneur, our passion lies in bringing together diverse talents of Bay Area local makers through our Marketplace events. Our events serve as an opportunity for eco-entrepreneurs, plant growers, specialty food makers, skilled artisans, and more to showcase their creations. Alongside our events, we offer HubOn transport to help makers reach customers outside their service area with our 50+ pickup locations across the Bay Area.

With a flourishing community of over 200+ makers, we believe in fostering sustainability by facilitating local transactions. We invite you to join us at our next event. Let's build connections, celebrate creativity, and grow together!

What is HubOn?

With HubOn, local makers can now make their products available for pickup at any of our 50+ store partners. We transport a variety of delicate goods from birthday cakes to kombucha to house plants, allowing makers to instantly serve the Bay Area without the need for a storefront.

Unlike online marketplaces, HubOn does not charge hefty commissions. We aggregate our deliveries through our hub partners reducing CO2 emission by 10X compared to door-to-door delivery services. We also hire and train W2 employees to ensure that every order is hand-delivered with the utmost care and attention.

Take a step towards sustainability and try HubOn transport for FREE today!
